# Getting Started

Welcome to our platform! Creating your own chatbot has never been easier. With no coding required, you can set up and manage chatbots tailored to your needs in just a few clicks. Here's how to get started:

## Sign Up and Get 10 Credits

1. **Sign up using Google**: Use your Google account for a quick and secure signup process.
2. **Free Credits**: Upon signing up, you'll receive **10 credits** to start creating your chatbots immediately.

## Navigate the Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll be directed to your **dashboard** where all your bots are displayed. The dashboard gives you a clear overview of your existing bots and allows you to manage them easily.

## Editing a Bot

1. Locate the bot you want to edit in the dashboard.
2. Click the **gear icon** next to the bot's name.
3. Select the **Edit** option from the dropdown menu.
4. You'll be redirected to the **bot-config page**, where you can update the bot's settings and features.

## Start Building!

You're all set to start creating and customizing chatbots. If you need any help, refer to the [API](./api.mdx) and [Appearance](./appearance.mdx) guides for more details.
